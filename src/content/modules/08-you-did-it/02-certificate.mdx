## What comes next

This course is a beginning, not an ending. Here is where to go from here:

**Keep practising.** Use AI at least once per week for a real teaching task. The habit builds quickly.

**Explore Gemini Advanced.** If you find yourself wanting more powerful capabilities, the paid version offers a more sophisticated model.

**Stay connected to other educators.** South African teachers are finding new AI applications every term. Your provincial subject advisor or teacher development centre may offer follow-up workshops.

**Revisit Module 7 regularly.** The Prompt Library, Glossary, and PARTS Reference are designed to be used again and again. They do not expire.

**Share what you know.** The most effective professional development is peer-to-peer. If this course helped you, tell a colleague. Show them one prompt. Let them see what is possible.
