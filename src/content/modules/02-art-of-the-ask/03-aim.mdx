## What do you want AI to DO?

The **A** in PARTS stands for **Aim** — the specific action you want AI to perform.

Your Aim always starts with an action verb. It is the clearest, most direct part of your prompt. No context, no explanation — just: "Do this."

---

## The power of the action verb

Compare these Aims:

- "Something about photosynthesis" — vague, no action
- "Explain photosynthesis" — better, but still open
- "Create a 10-question multiple choice quiz on photosynthesis" — clear and specific
- "Summarise the key steps of photosynthesis in a numbered list of no more than 8 steps" — excellent

The more specific your action verb, the more targeted your result.

---

## Action verbs for educators

Here are the most useful Aim verbs for teaching tasks:

| Task Type | Verbs to Use |
|---|---|
| Creating content | Create, Write, Draft, Design, Generate, Build |
| Simplifying | Simplify, Rewrite, Summarise, Condense, Explain |
| Checking/improving | Review, Improve, Edit, Enhance, Strengthen |
| Transforming | Convert, Translate, Adapt, Restructure, Transform |
| Brainstorming | Suggest, Brainstorm, List, Generate ideas for |
| Analysing | Analyse, Compare, Identify, Evaluate, Break down |
