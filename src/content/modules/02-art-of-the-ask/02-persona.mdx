## Who are you asking?

The **P** in PARTS stands for **Persona** — the role you want AI to take, and the context you bring as the teacher asking.

Telling Gemini who you are and who you want it to "be" transforms the quality of what it generates. A prompt from "a teacher" produces generic results. A prompt from "an experienced Grade 11 Afrikaans teacher at a rural school in the Eastern Cape whose learners speak IsiXhosa as their home language" produces something far more specific and useful.

---

## How to write a Persona

Your Persona has two parts:

**1. Who you are:**
Describe your role with relevant context. Grade level, subject, school type, and any specific circumstances that affect what you need.

*Examples:*
- "I am a Foundation Phase teacher at a no-fee public primary school in Limpopo."
- "I am a Head of Department for Mathematics at a high school in Johannesburg."
- "I am a Grade 4 class teacher with 42 learners in my class."

**2. Who you want AI to act as (optional but powerful):**
You can ask Gemini to adopt a specific role or voice in its response.

*Examples:*
- "Act as an experienced curriculum planner who understands the South African CAPS system."
- "Respond as a friendly, encouraging tutor who explains things simply."
- "Write in the voice of a strict but fair examiner."

---

## Why Persona matters

Without a Persona, Gemini assumes a generic audience — usually an international, English-speaking teacher with no specific context. For South African educators, this often produces output that:

- Uses American or British curriculum references
- Includes examples that are irrelevant to SA learners
- Defaults to "students" instead of "learners"
- Does not reference CAPS, DBE, or local context

With a strong Persona, you anchor Gemini to your reality.

<SAExample title="Persona in action">
**Without Persona:**
"Create a lesson plan on ecosystems."

*AI produces:* A generic 50-minute lesson plan using US-style curriculum language, with examples from temperate forests and American national parks.

**With Persona:**
"I am a Grade 7 Natural Sciences teacher at a public school in the Northern Cape. My learners are 12-13 years old and mostly speak Afrikaans and Tswana at home."

*AI produces:* A lesson plan with examples from Karoo ecosystems, terminology in accessible English, and activities suitable for multilingual South African learners.
</SAExample>
