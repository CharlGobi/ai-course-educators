## AI and your data budget

Data costs are a real concern for South African educators. Before you commit to using AI tools regularly, it helps to understand exactly what they cost in terms of data — and how to use them without breaking your data budget.

The good news: Gemini is one of the most data-efficient tools you can use. It uses far less data than streaming video, less than a voice call, and roughly the same as browsing a news website.

---

## How much data does Gemini use?

Here are approximate data costs for typical Gemini usage:

| Activity | Approximate data use |
|---|---|
| Sending a text prompt | Less than 1KB |
| Receiving a text response (1–2 paragraphs) | 2–5KB |
| A complete lesson-planning conversation (10 exchanges) | 30–50KB |
| Uploading a PDF to NotebookLM (10 pages) | ~200KB |
| A full afternoon's AI work (1 hour of prompting) | ~500KB–1MB |

**For comparison:**
- Loading a page on News24: ~500KB
- One minute of YouTube video (standard quality): ~10MB
- One minute of WhatsApp voice note: ~240KB

If you can load News24 on your data, you can use Gemini.

Google also offers Gemini Advanced as part of Google One. It costs approximately R229/month (pricing may change — check google.com/gemini/advanced for current pricing).

Gemini Advanced includes a more powerful AI model. For most teaching tasks in this course, the free version is more than sufficient. Advanced is worth considering if you:

- Work with very long documents regularly
- Need to generate complex data analysis
- Want priority access during high-demand periods

Do not feel pressure to upgrade. The free version is excellent for everything in this course.

## Should I use Gemini on school Wi-Fi?

Many South African schools have Wi-Fi networks, though access and speed vary. A few things to keep in mind:

- **Check your school's acceptable use policy** — some schools have policies about which websites staff can access on school networks
- **Gemini is a Google product** — if your school uses Google Workspace for Education, it is generally considered an approved Google service
- **Learner data protection** — do not use school Wi-Fi (or any network) to enter learner personal information into AI tools (see Module 6)

## The real cost calculation

Here is a simple way to think about it. If you spend 30 minutes using Gemini on mobile data, you might use approximately 0.5MB. At a typical South African data rate (roughly R5 per 10MB on a standard prepaid bundle), that is about R0.25 — less than the cost of a sweet from the tuck shop.

The return on that R0.25? Potentially a saved Sunday afternoon and a better lesson on Monday.

<SAExample title="Data-smart AI use">
Mrs Khumalo teaches in a rural school in Limpopo where connectivity is limited. She does all her AI prompting at home on the school's mobile data allocation each month.

"I do my AI work in two sessions a week — Sunday evening and Wednesday evening, at home on the school router. I generate everything I need for the week and save it to Google Drive. During the week I teach from what I saved. I have not gone over my data budget once."

Her tip: "Make a list of what you need before you open Gemini. Do not browse around. Go in, generate, save, and close."
</SAExample>
