## Plan ahead. Load shedding cannot stop a prepared teacher.

Load shedding is a reality of South African life. Power goes out. Wi-Fi routers go down. Mobile data becomes your only connection — or you may have no connection at all.

The teachers who get the most out of AI are the ones who treat it like a shop: they stock up when the power is on, so they are ready when it goes off.

---

## The core strategy: generate before the outage

You cannot use Gemini during load shedding without power or data. But you can use it before the outage to create a stockpile of ready-to-use materials.

Think of it as preparing lesson materials the way you might stock candles and a torch: in advance, not in the dark.

---

## Your generate-ahead checklist

Each week, spend 20–30 minutes when you have good connectivity generating materials you might need. Save them in places you can access offline.

## Scheduling your AI sessions around load shedding

Most South African schools receive a load shedding schedule from Eskom or their municipality. Use it to plan your AI working time.

**Before a scheduled outage:**
- Generate all materials for the next day's lessons
- Download offline documents in Google Drive
- Print any materials that need hard copies
- Screenshot key AI responses you want to reference

**During an outage:**
- Teach from your pre-prepared materials
- Use voice memos to capture ideas for later prompts
- Plan what you will generate when power returns

**When power returns:**
- Process your voice memos into proper prompts
- Generate the next bank of materials
- Back up your printed notes into digital form

<SAExample title="A bank of 40 backup lessons">
Mrs Pretorius has been teaching for 22 years and has lived through many years of load shedding. She spent one Saturday generating a "rainy day bank" in Gemini — 40 independent activities across her Grade 6 and 7 classes.

"I told Gemini: I need 40 pen-and-paper activities for Grade 6 and 7 Maths that learners can do independently during load shedding. Sort them by topic."

She printed the whole set and labelled the envelopes by topic. "Now when the power goes out, I reach into the right envelope and pass out the activity. My learners do not even notice anything different."

## Gemini's offline limitations

Gemini requires an internet connection to generate new responses. However:

- **Previously generated content** saved to Notes, Google Drive (offline mode), or printed is fully usable
- **Google Keep** syncs when online and is viewable offline
- **Google Docs with offline mode enabled** (in the app settings) are readable and editable without internet
- **Screenshots** are always available offline

The preparation habit is the key. Ten minutes of generate-ahead time saves you two hours of scrambling on a load-shedding day.
